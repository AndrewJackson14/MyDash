# Finding, editing, adding clients

**To find a client:** Go to **Sales → Clients**. Use the search bar at the top (matches on name). You can also click a client's name anywhere it appears in the app (pipeline rows, reports, ad projects) to jump directly to their profile.

**If a client isn't showing up**, check the filter chips at the top of the list — a status filter (Active / Lead / Prospect / Dormant) may be hiding them.

**Client profile tabs:**
- **Overview** — contact info, primary contact, status, total spend, last ad date
- **Timeline** — every interaction (emails, calls, comments, proposals, invoices) in chronological order
- **Sales History** — all closed and pipeline deals grouped by year, with contracts and standalone ads
- **Proposals** — sent, signed, expired
- **Ad Projects** — current and past creative work

**To edit a client:** open their profile, click **Edit Client** (top right of the profile header). You can update name, contacts, billing info, industries, interested publications, and notes.

**To add a new client:** on the Clients list, click **+ Client**. Name is required; everything else is optional but fill in what you know so commissions and reports work.
