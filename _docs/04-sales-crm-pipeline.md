# Sales pipeline — stages and the Follow-up column

The Pipeline tab shows every open opportunity as a card in columns representing deal stages:

1. **Discovery** — first conversation, needs assessment
2. **Proposal** — proposal sent, awaiting response
3. **Negotiation** — terms being worked out
4. **Closed** — won (becomes a sale), or moved to Cancelled
5. **Follow-up** — ads that ran in the last 5 days; reach out for renewal or feedback

**Moving a deal:** drag the card between columns, or open the card and change the Status field directly.

**"Follow-up" column** is special — it's not a pipeline stage per se. It auto-populates with Closed sales whose issue published in the last 5 days. Use it as a reminder to ask the client how the ad performed and pitch the next run.

**Assigning a rep:** each client has a Rep assignment. Pipeline is filtered by default to the logged-in user's clients (for salespeople) or shows all (for Publisher/admin). Toggle **By Rep** to see only your own deals.

**Stage revenue totals** show at the top of each column — the sum of deal amounts currently in that stage.
