# Flatplan — placing ads, locking pages

The Flatplan (**Production → Flatplan**) shows every page of an issue as a grid, with ads placed on specific page/grid-cell positions.

**Selecting an issue:** top of the page has a publication picker and an issue picker. Pick a pub, then an issue.

**Placing an ad:** the left sidebar shows **Unplaced** (sales with no page yet) and **Placed** (already assigned) cards. Drag an unplaced card onto a page. The system snaps to the nearest grid cell that fits the ad's dimensions.

**Tip:** use `⌘/Ctrl + click` on any ad card (in sidebar or on the grid) to jump to that client's profile without disturbing the drag.

**Re-placing:** drag a placed card to a new page/cell. Status flags update automatically.

**Removing:** click the small × in the top-right of the card on the page.

**Locking an issue:** once all ads + editorial are placed and approved, **Send to Press** freezes the flatplan and triggers the goal-allocation freeze (commission share % stops rewriting history). Everything on the flatplan stays in place; new sales can still get assigned to future issues.

**Placeholders:** dashed gray cards on pages represent reserved space (e.g. legal notice placeholders, editorial holds). Fill them in or edit their label inline.

**Shared pages across publications:** pubs with a "shared content" link (set on the Publication page) can share pages — so an ad placed once lands in both flatplans.
