# Signing in to MyDash

MyDash uses Google Sign-In with your 13 Stars email. Visit [mydash.media](https://mydash.media) and click **Sign in with Google**. On first sign-in the system matches your Google account to your team member record by email.

**If the email on your Google account doesn't match your team record**, you'll see a "No access" message. Ask MySupport to update your team record email, or add your Google auth ID manually.

Your role (Publisher, Salesperson, Editor, Office Manager, Graphic Designer, etc.) determines which pages you can see and what data is scoped to you. Salespeople see only their own clients and sales; Publishers and admins see everything.

**Session length:** you stay signed in until you explicitly sign out (Profile menu → Sign Out) or clear browser storage.

**If you're locked out**: (1) confirm you're using the same Google account as your team record email, (2) try an incognito window to rule out a cookie problem, (3) ping MySupport.
